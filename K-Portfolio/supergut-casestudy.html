Problem Statement

Required a user-friendly above-the-fold layout for quick scanning of image gallery and key product information.

Needed a clearer and more prominent presentation of the ingredient list as the most critical information.

Product page had to remain informative yet concise, avoiding excessive text.

Interactivity across the website needed improvement to create a more engaging and modern user experience.

The Approach

Replaced the image carousel with a structured image gallery for faster scanning and improved visual clarity above the fold.

Introduced dedicated testimonials and FAQ sections to build trust and reduce decision friction.

Designed a strong USP banner to clearly communicate product value at first glance.

Developed an interactive ingredient section to highlight key components without overwhelming users with dense text.

Added a comparison chart to simplify decision-making and showcase product differentiation effectively.
