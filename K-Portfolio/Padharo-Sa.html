WHY DID I CREATE THIS KIOSK

Where Tradition Meets Digital Dining

As a curator, I've meticulously fashioned each dish to honor the vibrant Rajasthani culture. You'll experience the essence of Rajasthan through vivid descriptions, captivating visuals, and even immersive sounds.

Stepping into my designer role, my main goal was to create a user-friendly kiosk experience — concise yet informative, modern yet rooted in tradition. The result is a seamless digital journey that enhances ordering while preserving cultural richness.
